## About Me

I help organisations understand and navigate the future of work — specifically how Agentic AI is fundamentally transforming software engineering.

With 25 years of experience across enterprise, startup, and academic settings, my focus is now entirely on the most profound technological shift I've seen: the transition to AI-native architectures. I am a technology leader and software architect who specialises in generative AI and cloud strategy, building scalable systems where human engineers and autonomous agents collaborate seamlessly.

I thrive on building the culture and environments needed to support this new era of engineering. My approach is entrepreneurial and customer-focused, with a strong bias for action. Whether I'm advising leaders on AI adoption or getting hands-on with Agentic workflows, my goal is to turn AI hype into tangible, proven success.

## Professional Experience

### Current Role — HCLTech AI Labs

I lead and mentor the EMEA AI Architecture team at [HCLTech AI Labs](https://www.hcltech.com){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}<span class="sr-only"> (opens in new window)</span>, shaping the end-to-end strategy, design, and implementation of Agentic AI solutions in a rapidly evolving space.

A major focus of my role is pioneering **Agentic Software Engineering**. We are fundamentally changing how software is built by using "2.5-person pods" — a shared architect, two engineers, and a team of coding agents. This model allows us to drastically accelerate the delivery of PoCs, MVPs, and pilots for both AI and traditional software, ensuring rapid delivery without sacrificing strong governance.

Technically, I serve as the lead SME for the Google Cloud ecosystem, building deeply with the Agent Development Kit (ADK), Vertex AI, and Gemini models. To maintain a pragmatic, industry-wide perspective, I also actively build and collaborate with OpenAI, Red Hat, and other key ecosystem partners.

### Previous Experience

Before HCLTech AI Labs, I was Director of Software Engineering at [Mastercard](https://www.vocalink.com/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}<span class="sr-only"> (opens in new window)</span> in London, responsible for cloud strategy and architecture for [real-time payment products](https://b2b.mastercard.com/real-time-payments/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}<span class="sr-only"> (opens in new window)</span> and building cloud native skills across architecture, engineering and DevOps teams.

Prior to that I was at HCL Cloud Native Labs, progressing from Cloud Native Solutions Expert to leading the IBM Red Hat Ecosystem and then to cross-ecosystem Head of Solutions Architecture.

Before industry, I spent eight years in Bioinformatics at the [European Bioinformatics Institute](https://www.ebi.ac.uk){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}<span class="sr-only"> (opens in new window)</span> in Cambridge, starting as a Software Engineer, moving into leading engineering teams, and developing my cloud skills. My focus there was software quality and developer productivity.
